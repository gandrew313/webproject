About

The Hofstra Men's Rugby team was founded in the year 1935, and has fielded a team every year since. During the fall semester, the team competes to win the championship of the Northern Division of the Tri State Rugby Conference. Every year in the spring, the team competes in the Long Island Cup and the Vassar 7's tournament, as well as many exhibition games.

Be a part of our community!

No experience is required to join our team, everyone is welcome! Rugby has a great culture behind it, giving many opportunities for new and experienced players to benefit themselves on and off the field.

Practice Times

Practice times are from 7-9 PM on the intramural fields, with occasional friday afternoon practices. When we aren't practicing, there is almost always at least 1 teammate working out in the fitness center. If the weather doesn't cooperate, we reschedule practice to be inside the fitness center.
